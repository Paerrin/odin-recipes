Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a lasagna recipe. It is delicious. MAKE IT NOWWWW!!!!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles: 1 box
  • Tomata Sauce: 1 jar
  • Sausage: 16oz
  • Cheese: All of it

Steps

  1. Turn oven on to 350
  2. Boil water in large pot
  3. Place pasta in boiling water
  4. Lay pasta in dish after cooking in single layer
  5. spread sauce and cooked sausage and cheese in layer
  6. Repeat until full
  7. Top with moar cheese.
  8. Bake for 45 mins
  9. let rest for 15 minutes
